Our Story

Selverleaf Edge Class was founded by the aged 19 Rym , a brilliant and creative student who was studying Mathematics . While her field was full of numbers, logic, and programming languages, Rym’s heart also beat for design, expression, and education.

She had a rare balance—the sharp mind of a coder and the soul of an artist. Even as she tackled complex equations and algorithms at university, she dreamed of building something beyond the academic world: a brand where learning feels alive, where students feel seen, and where creativity meets clarity.

She noticed how many learners struggled with traditional methods, and how boring and cold education could sometimes feel. That’s when she decided to combine her technical skills with her creative vision—and from that blend, Selver Edge Class was born.

She designed everything herself: the website, the lessons, the visuals—all while balancing lectures, projects, and code. Her goal was simple yet powerful, to create a space where people learn not only how, but also why—a space where growth feels beautiful, soft, and strong..

Today, Selver Edge Class stands as a reflection of Rym herself: smart, elegant, driven, and original. And it all started with one girl, a big dream, and a passion for both logic and art.

“Selverleaf” for the silver leaves that danced in her dreams, and “Edge” for the boldness she needed to bring her ideas to life <3

Our Mission

At Selverleaf Edge Class, we believe that language learning should be:

  • Personalized: Tailored to your goals, learning style, and pace
  • Practical: Focused on real-world communication skills
  • Engaging: Interactive and enjoyable to keep you motivated
  • Accessible: Available to anyone with an internet connection

We're committed to breaking down language barriers and empowering our students to communicate confidently in English for academic, professional, and personal success.

Our Teaching Philosophy

At Selver Edge Class, we follow these core principles in all our instruction:

Communication First

We prioritize practical communication skills over rote memorization, helping you use English confidently in real situations.

Student-Centered

Your goals and learning style guide our approach, with personalized feedback and adaptable lesson plans.

Positive Environment

We create a supportive atmosphere where mistakes are part of learning and confidence grows naturally.
